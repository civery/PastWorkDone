Process important activities
       Process measurement
o   You measure one or more attributes of the software process or product. These measurements forms a baseline that helps you decide if process improvements have been effective.
       Process analysis
o   The current process is assessed, and process weaknesses and bottlenecks are identified. Process models (sometimes called process maps) that describe the process may be developed.
       Process change
o   Process changes are proposed to address some of the identified process weaknesses. These are introduced and the cycle resumes to collect data about the effectiveness of the changes.
